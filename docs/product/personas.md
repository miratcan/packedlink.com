# Personas – PackedLink

This document outlines the current persona set and value proposition for each persona. Used to clarify revenue focus and audience expansion roles.

---

## 1. Affiliate Content Creator – "Sarah" (Revenue Persona)

**Profile**
- Shares product recommendations on Instagram / TikTok.
- DMs flooded with repeating questions: "Which baby stroller?", "What's your filming equipment?"
- Affiliate links scattered everywhere; can't track performance.

**Current Behavior**
- Keeps link lists in Notes app or Google Docs.
- Repeatedly sends same links via DM.
- Making do with story highlights and Linktree but no list logic.

**Pain**
- Can't present links organized, can't add notes.
- Affiliate parameters get lost, no click data.
- Answering same question wastes time, doesn't look professional.

**PackedLink's Value**
- Themed lists: "Baby Starter Kit," "Home Favorites," "Studio Equipment."
- One link → share everywhere, auto-updates when you edit.
- Pro plan roadmap: basic click stats, affiliate parameter protection.
- DM chaos ends; professional, pride-worthy guides.

---

## 2. Place Curator – "Mike" (Expands Audience)

**Profile**
- Person who constantly recommends restaurants/bars/cafes to friend circle.
- Keeps up with WhatsApp group messages: "Austin coffee?", "Brooklyn bars?"
- Gets lost among Google Maps lists or notes.

**Current Behavior**
- Searching through past WhatsApp messages.
- Trying to share Google Maps list but it's complicated.
- Using Notion/Notes but sharing is tedious.

**Pain**
- Sending same recommendation to five different groups again.
- No clean place to share link + short notes together.
- Hard to quickly edit/maintain on mobile.

**PackedLink's Value**
- Keeps "Austin Coffee," "Brooklyn Bars," "Date Places" in one link.
- Everyone checks same link, sees updates.
- List notes build trust with friends.

---

## 3. Resource Curator (Teacher / Trainer / Consultant) – "Audience Base"

**Profile**
- People sharing resource lists with students, team, or community.
- Want to centrally manage homework links, onboarding materials, course resources.

**Current Behavior**
- WhatsApp groups, Google Drive folders, email templates.
- Links get lost, everyone asks same question.

**Pain**
- Hard to keep shared links organized; need to explain from scratch for each new person.
- Difficult to quickly update and share on mobile.

**PackedLink's Value**
- Packs "3A class homework," "New employee onboarding," "Frontend resources" in one link.
- No entry barrier; people click link and consume immediately.
- Expands product base with free plan; long tail who might convert to Pro later.

---

## Persona Priority

1. **Revenue:** Affiliate content creator (Sarah) – Pro features shaped for this persona.
2. **Audience expansion:** Place curator (Mike) – represents "anyone can use" face of product.
3. **Community foundation:** Resource curator – main base of free users, spreads product awareness.

Hero message covers all personas: **"Pack your links. Share anywhere."**
Pro plan messaging tailored to Sarah, reference narratives to Mike/Resource personas.
